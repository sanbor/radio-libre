# Privacy Policy for LibreRadio

*Last updated: March 19, 2026*

## Introduction

LibreRadio is a free and open source internet radio player for iOS. It is built with privacy as a core principle. This privacy policy explains what data the app does (and does not) collect.

## Data We Don't Collect

LibreRadio does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

- No analytics or telemetry
- No crash reporting
- No advertising or ad tracking
- No user accounts or registration
- No usage statistics
- No device identifiers

## Local Storage

All data generated by your use of the app is stored **locally on your device** and is never sent to any server controlled by LibreRadio. This includes:

- **Favorites** — stations you mark as favorites
- **Recent stations** — your listening history
- **Settings** — your app preferences

This data remains on your device and is not accessible to anyone else. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed.

## Network Requests

LibreRadio connects to the [Radio Browser](https://www.radio-browser.info/) community API to retrieve station listings, search results, and station metadata. These requests are made directly to the Radio Browser servers. Please refer to the [Radio Browser FAQ](https://www.radio-browser.info/faq) for information about their data handling practices.

When you play a radio station, your device connects directly to that station's audio stream. LibreRadio has no control over the data practices of individual radio stations.

## Permissions

LibreRadio does not request any permissions beyond what is required for basic audio playback and network access.

## Open Source

LibreRadio is open source software licensed under GPL-3.0. You can inspect the complete source code to verify these privacy claims:

https://github.com/sanbor/libreradio

## Third-Party Links

Radio stations may have associated websites or links. LibreRadio is not responsible for the privacy practices of any third-party websites or radio streams.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected in this document with an updated date. As an open source project, all changes are publicly visible in the project's commit history.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
